Eid-Ul-Adha Mubarak! 🌙

عيد الأضحى مبارك
A gift of duas — for you
🌿

اللَّهُمَّ بَارِكْ لَهَا وَارْزُقْهَا خَيْرَ الدُّنْيَا وَالآخِرَة

On this blessed Eid, I wish you good luck for the rest of the year. May you never find a single tear in your eyes, and may Allah grant you everything you deserve — and so much more than you ever dared to ask for.

— A prayer from the heart

✨

اللَّهُمَّ اجْعَلْ يَوْمَهَا خَيْرًا مِنْ أَمْسِهَا

May every day ahead be brighter than the one before. May your smile never fade, your laughter never run dry, and may this Eid fill your home with warmth that stays long after the day is gone.

— Wishing you endless joy

🛡️

اللَّهُمَّ أَعِنْهَا وَثَبِّتْهَا وَقَوِّ قَلْبَهَا

When the world feels heavy and the road feels long, may Allah give you a strength you didn't know you had. May every hardship you face only reveal how unbreakable you truly are.

— May you always rise

🌙

اللَّهُمَّ اجْعَلْ قَلْبَهَا مُطْمَئِنًّا بِذِكْرِكَ

May your heart always find its stillness. May you carry a peace within you that no noise can disturb. May this Eid remind you that you are held — by faith, by love, and by every good dua ever made for you.

— A prayer for your inner calm

🌅

اللَّهُمَّ اكْتُبْ لَهَا النَّجَاحَ وَالسَّعَادَةَ فِي كُلِّ خُطْوَة

May your future be written in the ink of success and happiness. Every step you take — may it be guided, every dream you carry — may it be blessed, and every door that matters — may it open for you.

— For all the chapters still to come

— From Fatima, with sincerity 🌿
